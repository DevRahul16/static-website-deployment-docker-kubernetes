About Chelsea Football Club

Founded in 1905, Chelsea FC is one of England’s most successful football clubs, based in Fulham, London. Known as “The Blues,” the club has won multiple Premier League titles, FA Cups, and the UEFA Champions League.

Stamford Bridge has been the club’s home ground since its foundation, and Chelsea continues to inspire millions of fans worldwide with its spirit, loyalty, and excellence both on and off the pitch.
